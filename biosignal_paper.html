About & Disclaimer

What BioSignal Is

BioSignal -- A Biological Pathway Storyteller is a free, open educational tool that transforms a published scientific paper into a narrated, multi-scale biological cascade. It is designed to help students, educators, and researchers engage with primary literature by tracing the mechanistic pathway from a study's intervention to its reported findings.

AI-Generated Content

Biological cascades, narration scripts, and pathway descriptions are generated by artificial intelligence and may contain errors, omissions, or inaccuracies. Each slide carries a sourcing confidence indicator (paper-cited / pathway-inferred / extrapolated) and a direct link to the primary source. All content should be verified against the original paper before use in teaching, research, or any professional context.

Not Medical Advice

BioSignal is an educational tool only. Nothing generated by this tool constitutes medical advice, clinical guidance, diagnosis, or treatment recommendations. Do not use BioSignal output to make health or clinical decisions.

Your API Key & Privacy

BioSignal runs entirely in your browser. Your Anthropic API key is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to or stored by BioSignal. Paper metadata is retrieved via the Crossref and PubMed APIs under their standard open-access terms. No personal data is collected or retained by BioSignal.

Data Sources & Attribution

Paper metadata: Crossref and PubMed / NCBI.
Pathway data: Reactome Knowledgebase (Gillespie et al., Nucleic Acids Res 2022) · CC BY 4.0.
AI generation: Anthropic Claude via the Anthropic API.
